How secure is KathLock hashing algorithm ?


  • KathLock use its own hashing algorithm,so decryption of hash is not possible anywhere.
  • You will get a unique hash for the same password everytime.
  • The length of the hash is fixed for every password
  • KathLock's hash can only be decrypted on the hash owner's account
  • We do not store your plain text password anywhere
