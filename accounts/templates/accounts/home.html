How secure is KathLock hashing algorithm ?


  • KathLock use its own hashing algorithm,so decryption of hash is not possible anywhere.
  • You will get a unique hash for the same password everytime.
  • The length of the hash is fixed for every password
  • KathLock's hash can only be decrypted on the hash owner's account
  • We do not store your plain text password anywhere

How to encrypt ?


Go to the encrypt page.


Enter the name of the Passsword.
Enter the Passsword you want to encrypt.
Click on encrypt.


You will get your hash.
it is automatically saved in your "my hash", you can decrypt it or delete from there.


How to decrypt ?


Go to the decrypt page.


Enter your encrypted password.
Click on decrypt.

Note: you can decrypt your passwords directly from "my hash"
